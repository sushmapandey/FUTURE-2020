FUTURE 20/20 is a one of its kind platform curated to harness the growth developments of the SMEs and emerging businesses in the country. Being India’s ‘engine of growth’, small and mid-sized businesses have scaled significantly over the last few years with increasing awareness, digital advancements and expanding opportunities. With this initiative, Exito objectifies to discuss opportunities, advancements, challenges, innovations and solutions to propel the growth trajectory of emerging businesses.

India is bracing itself to be a $5 trillion economy by 2025 and the largest contribution to this milestone is anticipated from the SME & mid-market segments. The increasing engagement of emerging businesses on the mobile app and digital platforms has boosted the sector’s growth by over 60% in this year alone. Thus, heralding a harbinger of the movement towards applicable technologies and digitization.

At Future 20/20, the participants will create a growth-affirming narratives to motivate the rapid organizational evolution and emphasize the centrality of innovation.

Why SME?

SMEs play a very pivotal role in the development of national economy and contribute to inclusive growth, employment and trade. We at Exito, aim to curate a platform, helping SME founders & executive leaders who are the job creators at scale, strengthen their business through a vision to be India’s biggest information exchange platform for the SME sector. Future20|20 has been designed with a specialized focus towards empowering the sector for better business growth alongside a focus on the 4 pillars; leadership-driven innovation, growth strategies in the digital era, impact of emerging technology on business models, enabling changes in the policy landscape.

Future2020 is exclusively focused at the India’s vibrant SME sector aimed at enhancing their productivity, competitiveness and profitability. Attendees will gain access to a multitude of SME audience from Indian Mid-markets as well as an opportunity to build on strategic business relations and expansion opportunities.

In the recent past, the Indian Business eco system has evolved multi-fold and performed better than their larger counterpart. There are several factors that have propelled and contributed to such growth including generous funding by local and foreign investors; reduced trade barriers by trade directories and portals; and technology developments facilitating process orientation . Due to a significant contribution in terms of output, exports, and employment, the Mid Market now occupies a position of strategic importance in the Indian economic structure.

Future 20/20 is a must attend event that will highlight and focus on the future of the SMEs and Mid-market organizations and explore new technologies.
